( About Me )

I am a community-focused professional transitioning to architecture, driven by a commitment to contributing to equity-driven, sustainable, and innovative environments.

Leveraging a background in community development and recent certification in Digital Design & Visualization, I apply self-directed technical upskilling to solve complex design problems across professional and academic settings.

My goal is to combine rigorous industry experience with my passion for the built environment and community engagement.
